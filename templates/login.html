Welcome to the archaeography.nz research project. This is an experimental archaeological database project, intended to model the Site Recording Scheme (SRS) owned by the NZ Archaeological Association.

The aim of the project is to see what we can learn by subjecting the national archaeologal dataset to modern relational database techniques.

A good place to start understanding the project is the Introduction. Having read this, you may want to look at the manuals.

We keep libraries of geographic data to aid our archaeological analysis. Many of these came from Land Information NZ.

We also have a library of historic aerial photographs which we are georeferencing, to reveal archaeological information. These are sourced from Retrolens.
