Some guidelines to authors...

author & byline Author is your name in the format [surname]comma[initials]. eg. Smith, J.W. There should be a space after the comma and no spaces between the initials.
Byline is the name which will be printed as the author in display.
title & subtitle These appear as the headline and subheading in display, and as the listing heading and subheading. Make them brief.
status Required. Start with 'idea'. You can change it later. Status determines which list the article appears on.
series Name of the series this enty belongs to, if any.
embargo If this holds a date value, when published the article will list but not display. In place of the page content will be an embargo notice. If today's date is later than the embargo, this will have no effect.
byline The name which will appear in print as the author.
subject Keywords. A comma-separated list of subject headings.
coverage Comma-separated list of locations, ending with "new Zealand:.
headline Determines whether this article wil list in headliners or not. A positive value forces it onto the list, a negative value forces it off the list.
intro A short paragraphy which displays in long-frm list entries (like what goes on teh homepage).
copy This is the text content, with references to figure images, which will be displayed as the content of the article.
notes Any length text. Will display only to editors.
