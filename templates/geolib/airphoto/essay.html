The historic aerial photograph collection

This is a catalogue of relevent historical aerial photographic surveys. The surveys were flown mostly by NZ Aerial Mapping on contract for the NZ Government, from the late 1930s. The sets catalogued here cover the Waikato region, and archaeological sites are visible on many of the frames.

The collection has recently been made available online at retrolens.nz by a consortium of local government and Land Information NZ. We have downloaded frames covering areas of archaeological interest, and are undertaking a project to georeference them to sufficient precision to be able to measure visible archaeological features.
