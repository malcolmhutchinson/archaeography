Datasets held in our collection

Aerial photo frames

This collection holds metadata, and georeferencing information, on sets of historic vertical aerial surveys dating to the late 1930s. Recently made available by a consortium of LINZ and regional governments, these frames show evidence for hundreds of archaeological sites.

Cadastre (NZ property parcels)

Land information NZ hold the property parcels collecton. This function permits the search on parcel appellation, and presents a report detailing the archaeological sites within and adjacent to individual parcels.

Lidar elevation data

Land Information NZ supply selected sets of lidar elevation data. Many archaeological features can be seen in hillshade images from lidar data. We use lidar as a reference layer when georeferencing historic aerial photos, and we use it as background in our maps.

Orthorectified aerial imagery

Land Information NZ supply a large collection of contemporary true-colour orthographic imagery. We keep references to these sets to enable the easy selection of aerial image tiles against archaeological sites.

Topographic maps

We keep references to three series of topographic maps which have historic relevance to archaeology in New Zealand. The NZMS1 imperial series, and NZMS260 metric topographic map series are the basis of the Site Recording Scheme. These have been superseded by the Topo50 series.
