archaeography weblog

Occasional articles relating to computing and archaeology in NZ. Explanations of the various projects we are involved in here at archaeograpy.nz, thoughts on how we can use information technology to further the study of archaeology.

NZ archaeological bibliography

A searchable electronic index of archaeological literature relating to New Zealand. We index Archaeology in NZ, the NZJA, the JPA and the consultant's reports from Heritage NZ.

Geographic data library

This is our library of maps, plans, and historic aerial photography. These things contain an enormous amount of valuable information about archaeological sites, and we are gradually trying to extract as much of it as we can.

Archaeology in New Zealand

What can we learn from a national dataset?

The NZ Archaeological Association has been recording archaeological sites in New Zealand since 1957. There are nearly 70,000 records now held in the Site Recording System (SRS). We present some statistical analysis of this national collection.
