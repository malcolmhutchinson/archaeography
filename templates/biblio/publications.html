The collections

There are a small number of periodicals we endeavour to keep a comprehensive catalogue of in our bibliographic section. These are the newsletter of the NZ Archaeological Association (NZAA), and the two journals sponsored by the Association.

Publications (or unpublications) produced by both the NZAA and Heritage NZ Pouhere Taonga (HNZ) are represented in our index. HNZ keep the digital library of consultant's reports; the repository of NZ grey literature in New Zealand.

We have also tried to collect references for other substantial works on archaeology. These include the Department of Conservation's Science for Conservation series.

A work in progress...

Our intention is to provide direct links to the source documents where possible. Unfortunately, during the NZAA recently re-organised their website, meaning all our stored URLs are now obsolete. We are working on rebuilding them, and hope to bring this functionality to the site in the near future.
